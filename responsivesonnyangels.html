Sonny Angels

Sonny Angels are collectible miniature figurines from Japan, known for their charming and whimsical designs. Each figure features a small, naked baby-like character wearing a removable hat shaped like an animal, fruit, or seasonal theme, giving each one a unique personality. Originally created as blind-box toys, part of the fun is the surprise element, as collectors never know which figure they will get until they open the package. Over the years, Sonny Angels have gained a global following, appealing to both children and adults due to their cute, collectible nature and the creativity of their designs. They are often displayed in collections, used for photography, or simply admired for their adorable charm.
